description

The Trumpet is an online magazine which recalls the fashion paper world but also makes your user able to buy everything he/she sees online in few seconds. You will focus on the typographic choices for your logo and pages design and show the creative path that took you to the final version.

the concept behind

the logo

What makes a logo feel luxurious ? This was the question that we needed to answer while designing a typographic logo for TheTrumpet. Small caps and heavy tracking was my take on designing this logo.

defining my

typographic hierarchy

In order for the informations to be well structure, assigning particular fonts and variation was crucial.
Therefore, i chose ‘Gobold Extra1’ font family for the most important pieces of information then matched it with ‘ Barlow condensed ’ font family on the second level of the hierarchy.
Also, i played with the letter spacing, weight and line height to get some variation in the design.
