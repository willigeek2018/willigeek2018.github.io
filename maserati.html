Maserati

ART DIRECTION, VISUAL DESIGNER

description

Select an Italian brand that operates in the luxury, fashion or arts sectors, analyses the brand’s digital presence, its social media strategy and digital communication approach.

result

Present a preliminary concept for a digital campaign for your brand, using a medium of your choice: a mini-site, a social strategy, an email campaign …

roles

  • strategy
  • art direction
  • visual design

instructors

  • Andy Scupelli

project type

  • Individual

duration

  • nov 2017 - Feb 2018

Aquiring

insights

Maserati is definitely one of those Italian luxurious brands, but looking at their digital store, that sense of premium luxury wasn’t effectively conveyed. The experience was all like any other e-commerce out there. That was the insight for my concept.

coming up with

the concept

The concept that i came up with was to carry the user into a adventure-like as they go on buying their product in the maserati online store. The look and feel of the digital store was inspired by the sleek interior of the maserati car.
